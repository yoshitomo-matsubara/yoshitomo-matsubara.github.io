Dr. Yoshitomo Matsubara is an Applied Scientist at Amazon Alexa AI. He completed the Ph.D. program in Computer Science at University of California, Irvine (UCI) and worked on deep learning for resource-constrained edge computing systems with Profs. Marco Levorato, Stephan Mandt, and Sameer Singh. Before UCI, he obtained his Master and Bachelor degrees in University of Hyogo and National Institute of Technology, Akashi College (Previously, Akashi National College of Technology), Japan, respectively.

His main research interests are in machine learning, natural language processing, computer vision, information retrieval, and symbolic regression. For deep learning, his main interests are in knowledge distillation and supervised compression.
